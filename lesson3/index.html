Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to connect local businesses to customers that are looking for a fun event to take their family to. The commerce will come up with event ideas and bring in local businesses to fill the needs for the customers by providing items such as food and drinks, or fun games and activities. The site will help with attracting people to create a business opportunity for local businesses to expand their customer base.
