Target Audience

The target audience is anyone who is looking to take advantage of the local events. Laingsburg is a small town; however, offers a big spirt when it comes to local festivities. The events are suitable for all ages from young children up to senior citizens. If you like fun, you will not want to miss any of Laingsburg Funfest events.


Persona 1:


A person looking connect with other local families.


Ficitional Name:

Krista Cramer


Job:

Nurse at the local hospital


Demographics:

  • 43 years old
  • Married for 10 years
  • 3 children ranging from 3 to 9 years old
  • Looking for a fun place to take her kids on the weekends

Scenarios:

  • Krista is wanting to attend the carnival in the spring. She wants a fun place to take her kids that is nearby. The carnival is the perfect place for her kids to meet new friends.
  • Krista heard that Laingsburg Funfest will be hosting a pancake breakfast next month. It is a perfect opportunity to get out of cooking and cleaning up for a day.
  • The kids have been wanting to learn to fish. There is going to be a local fishing event hosted in the summer where adult fishermen are volunteering their time to teach the young ones how to fish.
  • A free overnight campout. This is the perfect event for the kids to learn more about the outdoors while Krista gets to enjoy a night to herself.
  • Baseball weekend is a great opportunity for the whole family to get involved in a sport and get out some of that energy in the kids.
